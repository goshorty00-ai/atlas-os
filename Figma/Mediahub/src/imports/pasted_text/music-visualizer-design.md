Around it:

* circular waveform rings
* bass pulse shockwaves

## 2. Spectrum Cathedral

A premium 3D hall of spectrum columns.

Not basic bars.

Use:

* tall translucent glass columns
* depth perspective
* reflective floor
* light beams
* bass columns at centre
* treble sparks at edges
* camera-depth blur

Looks like a sci-fi cathedral made of sound.

## 3. Waveform Engine

A mechanical/holographic engine powered by waveform ribbons.

Use:

* flowing waveform ribbons
* rotating turbine-like rings
* pulsing pistons of light
* left/right stereo lanes
* BPM tick markers
* waveform trails moving through the engine

Looks like music running through a machine.

## 4. Bass Core

A heavy bass-reactor mode.

Use:

* deep central bass orb
* subwoofer shockwaves
* low-frequency pressure rings
* screen-edge glow on kicks
* vibration distortion
* bass meter arcs

Looks powerful, not childish.

## 5. Neural Audio Map

A clean AI-style neural network, not random dots.

Use:

* structured node clusters
* album covers as nodes
* artist nodes
* genre lanes
* AI recommendation routes
* animated signal pulses travelling along paths
* clean labels that do not rotate awkwardly

Looks like AI analysing the music.

## 6. Lyrics Projection

A modern lyric projection mode.

Use:

* current lyric line large and crisp
* next lyric line smaller
* vocal frequency glow behind words
* beat-reactive typography
* word particles only around key words
* no spinning text

Looks like a futuristic karaoke/lyrics HUD.

## 7. Album Coverflow Pulse

A music coverflow visualizer.

Use:

* current album cover in centre
* related albums angled left/right
* covers pulse subtly to beat
* waveform ribbon underneath
* small Play / Queue / Lyrics controls
* audio-reactive background glow

Looks premium and useful.

## 8. Stage Light Grid

A concert lighting visualizer.

Use:

* grid of light beams
* laser sweeps
* beat-synced strobes
* moving stage panels
* volumetric glow

Looks like a live performance wall.
